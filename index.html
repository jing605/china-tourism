About China

China, a land of ancient wonders and modern marvels, offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. From the Great Wall to the vibrant cities, China's rich heritage and scenic landscapes promise an unforgettable adventure.

Top Attractions

Forbidden City

The Forbidden City in Beijing is a majestic palace complex that served as the home of emperors and the political center of Chinese government for nearly 500 years.

Terracotta Army

Discovered in 1974, the Terracotta Army in Xi'an is a collection of terracotta sculptures depicting the armies of Qin Shi Huang, the first Emperor of China.

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, with its towering sandstone pillars, inspired the floating Hallelujah Mountains in the movie Avatar. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-see natural wonder.

Guilin

Known for its stunning karst landscape, Guilin offers picturesque scenery with its limestone hills, winding rivers, and traditional villages.
